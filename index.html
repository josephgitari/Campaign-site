Meet Senator K'abisai
The People’s Champion

Born and raised in the heart of Kisumu County, Senator K'abisai knows what it means to fight for every opportunity. From the streets of Nyamasaria to the corridors of power, he has never forgotten where he came from — or who he fights for.

Check 100% Independent — No godfathers. Only the people.
Check Proven Track Record — Delivered water, roads, and bursaries in record time.
Check Youth & Women First — Over 60% of his initiatives target the next generation.
Check Zero Tolerance for Corruption — Clean hands, clear vision.

His Vision for 2027

A Kisumu County where every ward has clean water, reliable electricity, quality healthcare, and real jobs — not promises. Where your child’s future is not determined by which village they were born in.
